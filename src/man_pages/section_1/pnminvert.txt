NAME
       pnminvert - invert a portable anymap

SYNOPSIS
       pnminvert [pnmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads a portable anymap as input.  Inverts it black for white and produces a portable anymap as output.

SEE ALSO
       pnm(5)
