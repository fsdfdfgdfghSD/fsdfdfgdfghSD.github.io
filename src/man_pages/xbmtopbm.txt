NAME
       xbmtopbm - convert an X11 or X10 bitmap into a portable bitmap

SYNOPSIS
       xbmtopbm [bitmapfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads an X11 or X10 bitmap as input.  Produces a portable bitmap as output.

SEE ALSO
       pbmtoxbm(1), pbmtox10bm(1), pbm(5)
