NAME
       pgmhist - print a histogram of the values in a portable graymap

SYNOPSIS
       pgmhist [pgmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads a portable graymap as input.  Prints a histogram of the gray values.

SEE ALSO
       pgmnorm(1), pgm(5), ppmhist(1)
